file can't spoil a level you haven't beaten yet. Decode one only after a genuine
attempt (the three in-app hints come first!).

**To decode**, copy a block and run one of:

```bash
node -e "console.log(Buffer.from(process.argv[1],'base64').toString('utf8'))" "<paste block here, without line breaks>"
```

…or paste it into your browser DevTools console: `atob('<block without line breaks>')`
(join the wrapped lines back together first), or simply ask your AI assistant to
decode the level you're stuck on.
